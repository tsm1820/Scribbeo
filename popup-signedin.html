IMPORTANT NOTE : Please do not open/use Scribbeo on a Chrome 'new tab' ie the Chrome welcome page without any url, or on the Chrome extensions page. Doing so would result in errors.

Guide to using Scribbeo:

  1. Scribbeo only works on a page that is running a YouTube video
  2. Open a YouTube video on the current tab (not YouTube's home page)
  3. Click on the 'add new notes' button in the home page
  4. Add a title to your notes
  5. While the video is playing, type any note and click the 'arrow' button
  6. The note you have just typed will appear below
  7. Click on the note if you want to review the part of the video
